1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

Snappily enforces a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, and any content that exploits or harms minors. Our app is exclusively for users aged 18 and above.

2. Age Restriction and Verification

  • Users must be 18+ to register or use the app.
  • Age is declared during sign-up; suspicious profiles are flagged.
  • Manual review helps detect potential misrepresentation.

3. Preventive Features & Moderation Tools

  • AI Monitoring: All user-generated content is scanned.
  • Manual Review: 24/7 moderation team checks flagged content.
  • Profile Screening: Real photo checks and inappropriate image rejection.

4. Reporting & Response Protocol

Users can report content or profiles via in-app buttons. Reports are reviewed promptly. Confirmed violations are banned and forwarded to law enforcement.

5. Law Enforcement Collaboration

Snappily works with Interpol, local police, and INHOPE to report and act against CSAM. We comply with laws like COPPA, IT Act, and GDPR.

6. Staff Training

Our team receives annual training on child safety, abuse prevention, and best reporting practices.

7. Transparency and Enforcement

Repeat offenders are permanently banned. Logs are maintained for review and legal use.
